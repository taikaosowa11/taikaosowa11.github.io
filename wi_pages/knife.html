Custom Knife

Forged by Alex Kao-Sowa

My brother Alex forged this custom knife for me. It has around a 5 inch blade made of 1075 steel and a Mukwa handle. The end of the tang is exposed for use as a hammer, bottle opener, and grip retainer when doing chopping. We made the sheath winter of 2017, soaking full-grain leather and shaping it to the blade. The whole sheath is one piece of leather, held together with epoxy and some waxed thread.

A nice advantage of using the Mukwa wood (besides the familial heritage and its beauty) is that we have a bunch of it, so a lot of our tools match. Here a custom firesteel necklace I made and a gouge Alex forged have the same awesome Mukwa reddish-brown patterning (with some other tools made that day as well). Check out the gear page for more!
