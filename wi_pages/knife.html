Custom Knife

Forged by Alex Kao-Sowa

My brother Alex forged this custom knife for me. It has around a 5 inch blade made of 1075 steel and a Mukwa handle. The end of the tang is exposed for use as a hammer, bottle opener, and grip retainer when doing chopping. Next up is building the sheath for it so I can take it on trips.
