About

Hi! I'm Tai. The da Vinci project is something I created for myself in an attempt to broaden my knowledge and experiences while also pursuing depth of understanding. I'm a senior in college and primarily interested in sustainability and bioelectronics. I'm also interested in martial arts, writing, bushcraft and homesteading, classical guitar, ancient history, and other random subjects that keep life interesting. Da Vinci is the quintessential example of a polymath: a great example of exploring our time we have on earth to its fullest. I've tried to capture some of the things I'm exploring on this website, my Da Vinci project, to collect a bit of what I've learned and to reflect through writing. I also post some updates on a blog.
