About

Hi! I'm Tai. The da Vinci project is something I created for myself in an attempt to broaden my knowledge and experiences while also pursuing depth of understanding. I'm primarily interested in neuroelectronics and interfacing with the brain for both treatment and the development of new technology; the brain is the ultimate UI. I'm also interested in bioengineering, writing, martial arts, classical guitar, writing, and other random subjects that keep life interesting. Da Vinci is perhaps the most famous example of a polymath: a great example of exploring our time we have on earth to its fullest. I've tried to capture some of the things I'm exploring on this website, my Da Vinci project, to collect a bit of what I've learned and to reflect through writing. I post updates on my adventures on a blog, so if you're looking for live updates you can find them there.
