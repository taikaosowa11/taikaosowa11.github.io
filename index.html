The Da Vinci Project

About

Hi! I'm Tai Kao-Sowa, a sophomore at Stanford University studying bioelectrical engineering. The da Vinci project is something I created for myself in an attempt to broaden my knowledge and experiences while also pursuing depth of understanding. I'm primarily interested in neuroelectronics and interfacing with the brain for both treatment and the development of new technology; the brain is the ultimate UI. I also think we can pursue comprehensive understanding of a subject ithout sacrificing other pursuits for it. I'm also interested in computer science. And I want to spend time playing classical guitar, or competing in martial arts, or writing or reading or learning. Da Vinci did it, and I think we all can follow in those footsteps. I've tried to capture some of the things I like on this website, my Da Vinci project, to catalogue what I've learned and to reflect through writing.
