Lineage

2016–2020

I trained at Stanford BJJ, under Kevin Kopecky and Gene Folgo. I received my blue belt from Kevin in 2018 or 2019. I also received some instruction from Mark Tse.

2021–2022

I spent a year training with Romulo Melo in San Francisco. I received my purple belt in 2021.

2022–2025

I started training at JG Los Altos under Sang Mercado, my present coach.

2025–2026

This past year I've been traveling and dropping in at different gyms, although I'm still affiliated with JG Los Altos. I've had the good fortune of being welcomed at many gyms in and around San Francisco, including Black Sheep, Empire, Ralf Gracie Berkeley, Bay, Charles Gracie Richmond, and Cave Academy. This year I've been lucky enough to briefly train with Jozef Chen and catch rolls with Jackson Ngai and Mica Galvao which has been a huge honor.

Jiu Jitsu has been a practice, art, and community for the last ten years of my life and I'm exceedingly grateful.
